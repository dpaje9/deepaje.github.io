About me

A generalist
who goes deep.

I've spent nearly a decade making mission-driven work actually run: at Greenpeace, 350.org, the Hertie School, and Accountable Now.

I've organised international virtual retreats before Zoom was widely used, launched advocacy tools I'd never worked with before, and untangled the operational knots that quietly stall good work.

I'm a translator at heart: between strategy and execution, leadership and teams, and the tool and the human who has to use it on a Tuesday afternoon.

I'm also more technical than most ops people, and my years in product left a mark: I don't design perfect systems in a vacuum. We build a working version fast, see how your team really uses it, and improve it together from there.

The work I do best sits at the intersection of decision-making and delivery: close to whoever's steering, connecting strategy to the people, processes, and systems that carry it forward.
